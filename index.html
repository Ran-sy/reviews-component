  • New York City Break

    There are so many positives about this hotel - it felt disappointing that we were only using it as a base so we didn’t spend as much time there as we would have liked. The location is perfect. We were able to get the AirTrain from JFK and then the Subway to literally minutes walk from the hotel. It is minutes walk from Park Central and minutes walk from Times Square but when you walk out of the door it is not too busy or noisy that it is overwhelming. The staff here are attentive, efficient and very helpful. All areas that we used were spotlessly clean. We had a queen room and a twin double - both were big enough for what we needed, the beds were very comfortable and the rooms mix were quiet without us being able to hear external noise or noise from other rooms. We all had very good nights sleep. The communal areas are beautiful and nice to spend some time in. If I were to find fault here it would only be that you can have a little wait for the lifts. When we return to New York we will definitely stay here again.
